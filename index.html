BLACKOUT

BLACKOUT is a psychological-fantasy horror by Renee Pirdis. Darcy, also known as Panic, is a teenager who suffers from what she calls blackouts. These would take over her body, causing her to be almost possessed. She has no knowledge of these blackouts happening. With her friends' help they pursue a pathway to find out why they occur and some secrets along the way. BLACKOUT is a story, animation and a game
